Why 80/20 Thinking is the Key to Restaurant Growth

The Pareto Principle (80/20 Rule) states that 80% of your results come from 20% of your actions.

  • 80% of revenue comes from 20% of your menu.
  • 80% of repeat guests come from 20% of your audience.
  • 80% of your marketing impact comes from 20% of your efforts.
“Your restaurant doesn’t need more marketing. It needs a system that amplifies what’s already working.”

How to Identify Your 80/20 Growth Levers

Instead of chasing more social media followers, more menu items, or more ads, focus on what’s already working and double down:

  1. Analyze your top-selling menu items → Optimize pricing & upsells.
  2. Identify your most profitable guests → Target similar audiences.
  3. Leverage high-ROI marketing channels → Email & repeat customer incentives.

The Guest Data Flywheel

One of the most overlooked yet high-impact 80/20 levers is guest data collection. By building a guest data flywheel, restaurants can create predictable revenue streams:

Restaurants that own their guest data (emails, phone numbers, birthdays) have 10x the marketing power of those who don’t.
